Macro Performance Calculations
Metric 1: Average Order Value (AOV)
AOV = Total Sales Gross / Volume Processing Logs
Calculates aggregate transactional velocity across filtered criteria. Globally evaluated as: $139,399,439.65 / 1,000 entries = $139,399.44 baseline performance.
Metric 2: VIP Revenue Concentration Share
VIP Share = (∑ Sales from Tier 1 / Total Revenue) × 100
Measures structural reliance on high-net-worth customer matrices. Evaluated globally as 41.6% concentration velocity directly from audited data rows.
Metric 3: Sub-Segment Revenue Allocation Share
Cohort Share = (∑ Cohort Revenue / Total Revenue) × 100
Determines macro weight distributions of demographic cohorts (e.g., Middle Aged contributing $57.21M or 41.0%).
